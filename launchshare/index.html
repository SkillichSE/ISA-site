HOW IT WORKS

Three steps to orbit.

01

Log in with Discord

Enter your Discord username so the team can contact you.

02

Upload your contraption

Send the NBT file or the Contraption Diagram exported from the Create: Aeronautics axis. Only approved mods are allowed.

03

Wait for approval

ISA engineers review your satellite within 48h. If approved, your launch is scheduled and you get the date via Discord.

ORBIT TYPES

Choose your trajectory.

LEO

Low Earth Orbit

160–2000 km altitude. Fastest deployment, lowest cost, ideal for Earth observation and communication constellations.

Altitude160–2000 km
Period~90 min
SSO

Sun-Synchronous Orbit

Near-polar orbit that passes over the same spot at the same local time each day. Perfect for imaging missions.

Altitude~600 km
Inclination~97.8°
POLAR

Polar Orbit

90° inclination. Covers the entire Earth surface as the planet rotates beneath the satellite.

Inclination90°
CoverageGlobal
MEO

Medium Earth Orbit

2000–35 786 km. Used for navigation (GPS) and relay satellites with wider coverage areas.

Altitude2000–35786 km
Period2–24 h
GTO

Geostationary Transfer

Highly elliptical transfer orbit used to reach GEO. Apogee at 35 786 km, perigee in LEO range.

Perigee~200 km
Apogee35 786 km
GEO

Geostationary Orbit

Fixed position above the equator at 35 786 km. Ideal for broadcast and weather satellites covering large regions.

Altitude35 786 km
Period24 h
