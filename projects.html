PROJECTS

Missions &
Research.

Five active programs spanning launch systems, deep space research, orbital infrastructure, propulsion, and satellite systems.

ActiveLaunch Systems

Atlas

Development of next-generation launch vehicle architecture for ISA missions. Atlas encompasses rocket design, stage separation, and trajectory optimization for low and medium Earth orbit payloads.

PropulsionTrajectoryStage Sep
ActiveDeep Space Research

Horizon

Theoretical and simulation-based research into deep space mission profiles. Horizon investigates interplanetary transit windows, radiation shielding concepts, and long-duration life support frameworks.

InterplanetarySimulationLife Support
In DevelopmentOrbital Infrastructure

Titan

Design of modular orbital station components and docking systems. Titan focuses on habitat ring architecture, power systems, and attitude control for a future ISA orbital platform.

Station DesignDockingPower Systems
ActivePropulsion Engineering

Ares

Advanced propulsion concepts including electric ion drives, cold gas thrusters, and experimental hybrid engines. Ares produces engineering documentation and simulation models for ISA launch vehicles.

Ion DriveHybrid EngineThruster Design
In DevelopmentSatellite Systems

Nova

CubeSat-class satellite design, ADCS, and onboard computing for ISA's Launchshare program. Nova defines the technical specification all Launchshare payloads must meet.

CubeSatADCSOnboard Compute
