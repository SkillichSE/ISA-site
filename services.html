Launchshare

Satellite Launch Service

Book a slot on an upcoming ISA mission. Upload your NBT file or contraption schematic, choose your target orbit, and our engineering team will review your payload within 48 hours. Approved satellites are integrated into the next available launch manifest.

  • LEO, MEO, and GEO orbits available
  • NBT, SNBT, JSON, and image file support
  • 48-hour review turnaround
  • Mission slot confirmation via Discord DM
